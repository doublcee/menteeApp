About POP Network

Pursuit Of Purpose Network is living up to it's mission of raising exceptional leaders who will transform their families, organizations and communities globally with the launch of POP Network. This is a network where young Nigerian professionals meet with industrial experts.

This app comes with lots of interesting features, dual authentication for mentors and mentees, responsive interface, flexible profile updates, interactive community for mentors and mentees, real time job and scholarship update features in one click, direct messaging functionality, blogging features, goal scheduling and monitoring.
